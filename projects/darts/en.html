Controls

To close this window - press the cross at right-top corner of this window. Don't miss with common close button :). You can press Esc to close it too.

Before the game start you can select additional player by "+" buton

You can directly typed a scores numbert into player cells, use "TAB" to switch between cells. By switchin from last cell next player taking a turn.

Mouse left - to point a strike sector. If you miss then select a point - just take an arrow and drop it into right place.

Mouse right, button "»", " Space " - to switch turn to next player

button "«" - to undo turn results.
